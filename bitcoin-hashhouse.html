Bitcoin Hashhouse

Bitcoin Hashhouse is an informal network of bitcoiners who seek to live, work, and play in various locations around the world.

  • Connect with bitcoiners who have housing, coworking spaces, and other real estate.
  • Earn bitcoin by leasing your vacant condo
  • Work along side other bitcoiners to bring about hyperbitcoinization
  • Temporarily swap your place for another amongst bitcoiners
